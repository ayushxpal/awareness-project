FAQ

What are solar subsidies?

Solar subsidies are financial incentives provided by governments or other organizations to promote the adoption of solar energy.

How can I apply for solar subsidies?

The application process for solar subsidies varies by location. You can usually find information and application forms on government websites or by contacting local authorities.
